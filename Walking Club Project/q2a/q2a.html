Welcome to the OU walking club.
We are an enthusisatic group of open university walking students and staff taking in the walks and sights around the UK.

Take a walk across the beaches of northumberland, across the pennines, or a walk around town, surely you will be eager to see what is out there.
Check out the latest walk events or suggest a walk to the club.

Get those boots on and we hope to see you soon.
